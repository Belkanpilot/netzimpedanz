Making Power Grids Smarter

We are a coalition of engineers from industry and research with the common goal of making power grids and systems better, smarter, and more economical.

View our Goals

Objectives of the Interest Group

Creating Understanding

Promoting understanding of the decisive role of grid impedance within the context of the energy transition.

Discussion Forum

An open forum for exchange on the digitalization of electrical grids and modern measurement methods.

Decentralized Supply

A platform for discussions on the current challenges of a decentralized and stable energy supply.

"We do not see grid impedance as an end in itself. Knowledge of grid impedance makes a significant contribution to the success of a decentralized energy supply."
